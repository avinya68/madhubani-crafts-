About Us

Handpainted with love,
from Bihar to your door

Madhubani Crafts brings you authentic Mithila art — an ancient tradition that dates back over 2,500 years. Every piece is meticulously handpainted by skilled artisans using natural motifs of peacocks, fish, flowers, and gods from Mithila mythology.

We paint on sarees, kurtas, canvas paintings, home decor, and accessories — making wearable and liveable art that carries the soul of Bihar into your everyday life.

When you buy from us, you directly support the artisan families who keep this tradition alive.
